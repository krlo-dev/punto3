This page is about my favorite games

By: Carlos Rodriguez

Name of the video game: Minecraft

Video Game Summary:Minecraft is an open-world game where players can explore, collect resources, and build structures in a randomly generated environment. Players have complete freedom to create and modify their environment using three-dimensional blocks representing different materials such as earth, stone, water, and more.

Name of the video game: Valorant

Video Game Summary: Valorant is a first-person shooter (FPS) game developed by Riot Games, focused on competitive multiplayer. In Valorant, two teams of five players face off in matches where one team attempts to plant a bomb (called a "Spike") and the other team must defuse it or eliminate the enemy team.

Name of the video game: Fortnite

Video Game Summary: Fortnite is an action-survival video game developed by Epic Games that combines elements of building, shooting, and exploration in an open-world environment. The most popular mode is Battle Royale, where up to 100 players compete on an island to be the last one standing, whether solo, duos or teams. Players must explore, collect weapons, resources, and build defensive structures as the safe zone of the map shrinks over time, forcing players to engage in confrontation.

Name of the video game: Hades

Video Game Summary: Hades is a roguelike action game developed by Supergiant Games. The player takes on the role of Zagreus, the son of the god Hades, who seeks to escape the Underworld and reach Mount Olympus. Throughout the game, Zagreus fights hordes of enemies and faces challenging bosses while exploring different levels of the Underworld, which change with each match due to its roguelike nature
